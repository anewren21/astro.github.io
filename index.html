Astrology

Birth Charts

A birth chart is a map of the sky at the exact moment of your birth. It shows the positions of the planets, moon, and sun in the twelve astrological houses.

Sun Signs

Each sun sign is associated with a specific date range and symbol. The twelve signs are:

  1. ♈ Aries
  2. ♉ Taurus
  3. ♊ Gemini
  4. ♋ Cancer
  5. ♌ Leo
  6. ♍ Virgo
  7. ♎ Libra
  8. ♏ Scorpio
  9. ♐ Sagittarius
  10. ♑ Capricorn
  11. ♒ Aquarius
  12. ♓ Pisces

Your sun sign represents

  • Your personality: basic traits, motivations, and how you express yourself
  • Identity: Your core essence and the part of your personality that's most visible
  • Drive: Drive, ego, and pride
  • Attitude: Your general outlook and approach to life
